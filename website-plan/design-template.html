Project plan

Title

Digital Retreat

Goal

To serve as a place for my thoughts and web-dev creations, as well as other remembrances.

Target Audience

Primarily myself. Close friends, close family. Anyone looking for something random to read.

Main Features

Primarily just text with some images. Some web-designs and related ideas.

Personality

The mood of the site will hopefully be over all relaxing and hopefully intriguing.

I'm hoping there will be enough intersting content here for people to stop and spend some time reading/viewing and analysing the site's content.

Limiting Factors

Since this project (or a future reiteration of it) will stretch far beyond this quarter, I am a little staggered by this question. But the current limitations are the site interactivity. While I can easily update content by editing the source code, I'd like to eventually be able to update it through the website itself (filling out and submitting forms, uploading pictures through a form or even a drag and drop.) but I believe that requires some back-end abilities. I also don't have the skillset to be able to divvy the site up between people not logged into a created account, and those who are. Say for example, I wanted a section of it to be more personal, and didn't want it posted on the public web, but still wanted it on the site. I could have "private" entries only logged-in users could view.

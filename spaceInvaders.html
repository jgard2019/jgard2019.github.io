Project Details

Coding simple games is a great way to take a break from the more complex projects, while still practicing coding. I was a little surprised by now much trouble this little game gave me once I tried to finalize and debug. The challenges were mostly about getting things to work perfectly, more than getting them to work in the first place. One of my bootcamp instructors warned us, and said something to the effect of..

"You'll spend 10 percent of your time coding 90 percent of your project, and 90 percent of your time coding the last 10 pecent"

Credit where it's due. This was a code-along project I found on YouTube but I truly tried to make it my own and did not copy and paste anything.

Video here.

I'm still adding a few finishing touches and bug fixes.
